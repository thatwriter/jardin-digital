## Build your calmest and happiest place in your own home
When your inspiration comes from a place that is close to you, that place becomes your safe place, too. It's a place to create, to live in, and you can even invite other people to share it with you.

This special dream place is very important. When everything in it inspires you, it helps you get into a creative mood. This is perfect for making a new piece of art, thinking of a concept for a show, or doing anything that needs creativity.

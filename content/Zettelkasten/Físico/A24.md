## The colour Red can be used as symbol of humanity
It is the same color as the heart, as blood, as pure emotion itself. It is used as a reflection of our inner state: the tragedy, the suffering, the drama. That is when a symbol, like a red nose, shows our outer expression of ourselves.

It is the vulnerability of being human, expressed on the face. We use our own bodies to say the things we don't usually say out loud: "WE FEEL."
